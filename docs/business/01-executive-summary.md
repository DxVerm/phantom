# PHANTOM Protocol - Executive Summary

## One-Liner
PHANTOM is the first blockchain to combine Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) with post-quantum cryptography, enabling truly private transactions that remain secure against quantum computers.

---

## The Problem

### Today's Blockchains Have Two Critical Flaws:

1. **Zero Privacy**: Every transaction on Ethereum, Bitcoin, and most blockchains is publicly visible. Your salary, purchases, and financial history are exposed to anyone.

2. **Quantum Vulnerability**: Within 10-15 years, quantum computers will break the cryptography securing $2+ trillion in crypto assets. No major blockchain is prepared.

---

## The Solution: PHANTOM

| Feature | How It Works | Why It Matters |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| **FHE Encryption** | Transactions computed on encrypted data | Validators process transactions without seeing contents |
| **Post-Quantum Signatures** | Dilithium (NIST-approved) signatures | Immune to Shor's algorithm attacks |
| **Zero-Knowledge Proofs** | Groth16 + Nova recursion | Verify without revealing data |
| **Encrypted State** | ESL (Encrypted State Ledger) tree | Account balances remain private |
