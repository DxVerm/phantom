## PRESENTATION TIPS

1. **Keep it to 15 minutes** - VCs have short attention spans
2. **Lead with the problem** - Make them feel the pain
3. **Demo if possible** - Show the tests passing, light client working
4. **Know your numbers** - Be ready for financial questions
5. **Be honest about gaps** - "We need a co-founder" is OK to say
6. **Practice the quantum story** - It's your unique angle
7. **Have a clear ask** - "$3-5M for X milestones"
