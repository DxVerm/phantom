## Cost-Saving Strategies

1. **Remote-first team** - No office costs
2. **Grants first** - Non-dilutive capital
3. **Open source tools** - Minimize SaaS spend
4. **Community developers** - Grant program for features
5. **Bug bounties vs full audit** - Phased security approach
6. **Conference speaking vs booths** - Free marketing

---

## Financial Red Flags to Avoid

- ❌ Spending on marketing before product works
- ❌ Large office/equipment before revenue
- ❌ Hiring executives before IC contributors
- ❌ Expensive legal before necessary
- ❌ Token launch before utility exists
- ❌ VC money before product-market fit signals
